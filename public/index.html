Why we do it
2017-2018 Surveys conducted by the USBLS, Forbes and Business Insider, revealed that personal and professional references were the Largest factor in a company's final decision to hire an applicant.
But in a world where workers are increasingly mobile and relocating due to educational and technical job training, it has become increasingly difficult for newer applicants to build reliable and dependable long-term references.
Social Networking For Professional Growth
No more wasting time making connections in a new area with people who aren't serious about building a network of positive contacts for professional growth. We are focused on helping each other to connect to find a good job and to grow, personally and professionally.
